ABOUT ME

Hey, I'm Ilai

THIS IS MY STORY.

2019

MY LIFE BEFORE TRAINING

I grew up always doing sports — soccer, surfing, skateboarding. Movement was always part of my life. Then I had a collision in the water — someone was in a place he shouldn't be and I hit and injured him. It took the joy out of it overnight. I didn't get hurt, but something changed. I stopped going out surfing and spent the next stretch just staying home, playing video games.

2019 — The Turning Point

ONE CLASS CHANGED EVERYTHING

Eventually I got tired of it. A friend dragged me to go with him to a CrossFit class. Before it started, there was a conditioning session run by a gymnast — and we worked on handstands. I figured it would be easy, maybe something you learn in a week or two. I mean, who didn't try to do handstands on the beach as a kid right? It wasn't easy at all. That gap between how easy I thought it would be and how hard it actually was completely hooked me.

📸 Photo coming soon
2019–2020 — The Obsession

FIRST FREESTANDING HANDSTAND

Four to five months later I had my first solid 10 second freestanding handstand. More importantly, I had a coach who changed how I thought about training — and about life. The discipline, the mindset, the process. I decided I wanted to give that to other people too.
