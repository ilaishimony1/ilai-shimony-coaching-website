My Story

THE JOURNEY

2017 — The Beginning

FIRST STEPS IN CALISTHENICS

Started training with bodyweight movements in a local park. Couldn't do a muscle-up, couldn't hold a handstand for a second. Just a lot of failing and getting back up.

2019 — The Turning Point

OBSESSED WITH HANDSTANDS

Discovered the handstand world and became completely obsessed. Started training 6 days a week, deep-diving into technique, balance mechanics, and wrist conditioning.

2021 — Going Deeper

MOBILITY & MOVEMENT

After dealing with my first serious injury, I rebuilt my training from the ground up with a focus on mobility and movement quality. It changed everything about how I train and teach.

2022 — Sharing the Knowledge

STARTED COACHING & CONTENT

Began creating tutorial content online and taking on my first coaching clients. Realized that helping others progress gave me more satisfaction than any personal skill milestone.

2024 — Building the System

FULL-TIME COACHING

Went fully into online coaching — built my own coaching app, refined my programming methodology, and have now worked with athletes from over 15 countries.
